About

Connectir is an R package to conduct various advanced functional connectivity analyses such as Connectome-Wide Association Studies (CWAS) using Multivariate-Distance Matrix Regression (MDMR) or CWAS-MDMR. CWAS-MDMR attempts to find regions of the brain with connectivity patterns that are significantly associated with a phenotype. For instance, if you have two groups (ADHD and Controls), then CWAS-MDMR would find regions whose connectivity patterns significantly differentiate between the two groups.
